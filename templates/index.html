🌱 Vegetation Products

🌱 Vegetation Products

Vegetation products are used to monitor crop conditions, vegetation health, drought stress and biomass productivity.

NDVI

NDVI measures vegetation greenness and plant vigor using satellite imagery.

NDVI long-term average

Baseline represents long-term average vegetation conditions for comparison against current observations.

NDVI anomaly

Anomaly shows deviations from normal vegetation conditions and helps identify drought stress.

Vegetation Condition Index (VCI)

VCI indicates vegetation drought stress by comparing current vegetation conditions against historical extremes.
