🌱 NDVI (Normalized Difference Vegetation Index)
NDVI measures vegetation greenness and health. Values range from -1 (water/clouds) to +1 (dense green vegetation).
Useful for crop condition and drought monitoring.
Clip
📊 Long-Term Average NDVI
Historical baseline (multi‑year average) used to compare current vegetation against normal conditions.
Clip
⚠️ NDVI Anomaly
Deviation from the long-term average. Negative values indicate vegetation stress / drought, positive values suggest above‑normal greenness.
Clip
💧 VCI (0–100)
Compares current NDVI to historical min/max. Values < 35 indicate severe drought stress, > 65 show healthy vegetation.
